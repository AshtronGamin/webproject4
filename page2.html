Naawi-Oodena

Why We Are Building It

Naawi-Oodena is being built to restore land, culture, and community. It represents a historic agreement between Indigenous nations and the Government of Canada to return control of a significant urban space. This development serves as a symbol of reconciliation, resilience, and renewal—providing housing, economic opportunity, and cultural revitalization in the heart of Winnipeg.

How We Are Building It

Naawi-Oodena is being built through a collaborative, community-led process. Planning is directed by Indigenous leadership and guided by values like sustainability, accessibility, and cultural inclusion. The design prioritizes walkability, environmental protection, and mixed-use development, blending residential, recreational, and educational spaces. Every step—from naming streets to selecting building materials—is rooted in respect for people and land.
