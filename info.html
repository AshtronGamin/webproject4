Discover Naawi View

Residential Living

From peaceful single-family homes to mid-rise apartments, our residential zones are built for balance. Nature and neighborhood come together here, offering a place for all generations to grow and thrive.

Commercial Mixed-Use

Shops, offices, and cafes blend into residential living in a smart, walkable design. Everything you need is right around the corner—whether you're grabbing coffee or heading to work.

Community Spaces

These are the heart of the community—places where kids play, elders relax, and stories are shared. Open lawns, seating zones, and event areas give everyone a space to belong.

Sports & Recreation

Get active and stay connected. From football fields and skateparks to forest trails and open gyms, Naawi View keeps your body and mind moving in the right direction.

Cultural Campus & Education

Learning lives here. With a focus on Indigenous knowledge, creative arts, and modern education, the Cultural Campus is where identity and imagination come together to shape the next generation.

Mixed-Use Village

This is the soul of Naawi View—a vibrant village core full of people, culture, food, and fun. It’s where traditions meet new ideas and where you’re always a short walk away from something exciting.
