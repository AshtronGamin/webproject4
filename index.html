Naawi-Oodena

Where Is It Built

Naawi Oodena is located on the gentle edge of the Whispering Valley, just north of the serene River Kweyetun. Surrounded by old-growth forest and rolling hills, the neighborhood is about 15 minutes from the nearest town center but feels like a world of its own. The land it was built on holds deep cultural and spiritual significance, once used as a seasonal gathering place for storytelling, healing, and celebration. Its name, meaning "the center of the four winds" in an old local dialect, reflects its role as a meeting place for people, cultures, and ideas. Well-connected by sustainable transit routes and walking trails, Naawi Oodena is both peaceful and accessible.

Why We Made It

Naawi Oodena was envisioned as a community where people could feel truly connected to both nature and each other. We aimed to create a neighborhood that reflects harmony, inclusion, and sustainability. By blending traditional values with modern living, we sought to offer something unique and meaningful. Our goal was to establish a safe and inspiring space for future generations to grow and thrive.
